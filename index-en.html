📍 Must-see destinations

The best places in Bogotá

From the historic heart of La Candelaria to the heights of Monserrate Hill, Bogotá has a unique cultural and heritage richness.

History
La Candelaria

The historic center of Bogotá, with its cobblestone streets, colorful colonial houses and architecture that tells centuries of history.

⏱ 2–3 hours🎫 Free entry
Culture
Monserrate Hill

Iconic sanctuary at 3,152 meters altitude. Offers a spectacular panoramic view of the entire city and access by cable car or funicular.

⏱ 2–4 hours🎫 $28.500 COP
Art
Botero Museum

Free entry to enjoy the collection of master Fernando Botero and works by Picasso, Monet and Dalí in the heart of La Candelaria.

⏱ 1–2 hours🎫 Free
Culture
Gold Museum

The largest collection of pre-Columbian goldwork in the world. More than 55,000 pieces in gold and tumbaga from Colombian indigenous cultures.

⏱ 2–3 hours🎫 $4.000 COP
Gastronomy
Zona Rosa & Usaquén

Modern neighborhoods with the best restaurants, specialty coffee shops and Sunday flea markets. The cosmopolitan face of the capital.

⏱ 2–3 hours🎫 Free entry
Nature
Simón Bolívar Park

The green lung of Bogotá. 361 hectares with lakes, bike paths and cultural spaces. Ideal for a family Sunday outdoors.

⏱ 1–4 hours🎫 Free
